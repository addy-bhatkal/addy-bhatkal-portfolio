I created this model with a 'Support Vector Machine Classifier' algo on a dataset of 2.3k customer purchases in a superstore

- I tested this using models like Support Vector machines (accuracy: 83.25%) Gradient Boost Classifier (accuracy: 82.07%), Decision Tree Classifier (accuracy: 80.81%), Logistic Regression Classifier (accuracy: 81.38%)
- I choose this model because of its high accuracy score compared to other models above and for a consistent accuracy score across multiple combinations/splits of data. (Cross validation)

I used Recursive Feature Elimination (RFE) to identify the most important features/predictors .
- This happens by testing the model by recursively elimintaing one predictor after another to get the best predictors
- While in a model like SVC it is sometimes difficult to predict which predictors had more influence than the others the above combination of 11 predictors gave the highest accuracy score
